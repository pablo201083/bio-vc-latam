# Source-Backed Batch Strategy

Generated at: 2026-04-20 18:50:09

## Why larger batches

- Use large batches for `source acquisition` only: find external URLs and minimal evidence fast.
- Use smaller follow-up passes for `editorial hardening`: refine summary, scope note and taxonomy adjustments.
- This keeps quality high while increasing throughput.
